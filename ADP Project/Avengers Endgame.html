AVENGERS ENDGAME

(Avenge The Fallen)




Directed by: Anthony Russo,Joe Russo

Produced by: Kevin Feige

Release date: April 26, 2019

Running Time: 181 minutes


Storyline:

After the devastating events of Avengers: Infinity War (2018), the universe is in ruins due to the efforts of the Mad Titan, Thanos. With the help of remaining allies, the Avengers must assemble once more in order to undo Thanos's actions and undo the chaos to the universe, no matter what consequences may be in store, and no matter who they face...

Review

As I am a great fan of marvel Avengers: Endgame, which marks the end of the Infinity Saga, is spellbinding and surely an enthralling experience. It is remarkable and doesn't disappoint. Watching all our favorite superheroes in one film is just surpassing.The film has a great balance of humor, emotions, and action. The biggest strength of the film is the emotions. This is the most emotional superhero film I have ever seen. It's just perfect. The action sequences were jaw-dropping. The climactic battle left me amazed. It's just filled with memorable moments and cannot be described with words. The visuals are gorgeous and have a great impact on the film. The background score gives me goosebumps, though I've listened to it several times.


Ratings: 8.5/10
